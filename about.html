My Journey

Hi, I'm Vincent Vertolicha, a junior front end developer from Indonesia 🇮🇩. I graduated with a degree in Management, a major centered around achieving organizational goals effectively and efficiently. It is far from the world of web development and lines of code. Toward the end of my studies, my life was split three ways at once, finishing my bachelor thesis, helping my family's business, and somewhere in between, I tried building a website. Turns out, it clicked.

There was a different kind of satisfaction watching something that only existed in my head come to life on a screen. But time and energy have their limits. I chose to finish my two main responsibilities first, my bachelor thesis and helping my parents.

Not because I gave up on my passion, but because I believe everything has its right time. After graduating, it was finally my turn to focus. Now I'm fully diving into web development, and this is just the beginning. My goal is clear, to become a full stack developer. There's still a lot to learn, and honestly I'm enjoying every part of the process to achieve my goal.
